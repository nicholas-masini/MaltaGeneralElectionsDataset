- PSIZE = PTOTAL/VOTE * 100
- QSHARE = CT1/QUOTA * 100
- PSHARE = CT1/PTOTAL * 100
- TOPS = Max number of counts from all counts for that canditate
- LAST = Canditate's total number of votes for last count
